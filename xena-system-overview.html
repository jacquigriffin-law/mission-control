Trust & Security
Built for a law practice
Xena was designed with legal professional privilege, client confidentiality, and compliance obligations at the core — not bolted on as an afterthought.

🔒
Data stays on your machine

All data lives on your Mac Mini. Nothing is sent to AI training pipelines. Zero data retention on API calls. Full disk encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit.

⚖️
Legal professional privilege

Client data is never shared between matters. Conflict checks run before any new intake. No client names in system logs — matter numbers only.

📋
Compliance logging

Every conflict check is logged with timestamp, names searched, result, and action taken. Append-only log — can never be deleted. 7-year retention as required by NSW Law Society.

✅
Human approval required

Xena never auto-sends emails — everything goes through a draft queue for your review. Court date changes never auto-modify your calendar. You always have the final say.

🛡️
Sandboxed access

Xena cannot see other systems on the same machine. Each client's data is completely isolated. SSH access is restricted. API keys have minimum required permissions.

📚
Regulatory compliance

Designed to comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988, Legal Profession Uniform Law (NSW), Australian Solicitors' Conduct Rules, and the Family Law Act 1975.
